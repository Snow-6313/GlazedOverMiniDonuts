This Privacy Policy explains how Glazed Over Mini Donuts may collect, use, and protect information when you visit this website.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect information in a few different ways depending on how you use the site:

  • Information you provide voluntarily, such as your name, email address, phone number, event details, or message if you choose to contact us.
  • Preference information, such as your theme and language settings, when you allow optional preference storage.
  • Consent information, so the site can remember your cookie and storage choices.
  • Basic technical information that may be handled by your browser or third-party services needed to load website content, fonts, or social links.

2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • respond to inquiries and event or catering requests;
  • operate and improve the website experience;
  • remember your privacy, language, and theme preferences when permitted;
  • keep the site secure and functioning properly; and
  • update website content and customer-facing information.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses a consent record that is necessary to remember your privacy choice. If you accept preference storage, the site may also save your selected theme and language settings in local storage.

By default, the site does not load advertising or analytics cookies through the consent manager currently implemented on this website.

4. Contact Forms and Messages

If you choose to contact us through a form, email, or social media, you may share personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, event information, and message contents. We use that information only to respond, communicate, and help with your request.

5. Third-Party Services and Links

This website may use third-party services or resources to operate, including hosted fonts, dynamic content services, and links to social media platforms. When you leave this site or interact with a third-party service, that third party's own privacy practices will apply.

  • Google-hosted font resources may be requested by your browser.
  • Dynamic website content may be loaded through Firebase services.
  • External links to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or other websites are governed by those services.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information through this website. We may share information only when needed to run the site, respond to a request you make, comply with the law, or protect our rights and safety.

7. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was provided, to respond to inquiries, to maintain business records, or to meet legal obligations.

8. Your Choices

You can manage optional website preference storage through the cookie settings interface on the site. You can also avoid submitting personal information by not using contact methods on the website.

9. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended to collect personal information from children knowingly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown on this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how the website works, please contact us through the contact page.
